## 1. Overview

**Pitch:** EpicWar is a 2D castle-vs-castle strategy game built in Godot 4.x, inspired by the Epic War flash game series. Players build a deck of units before battle, then watch their army auto-march across a single-lane battlefield to destroy the enemy castle while defending their own. The core fantasy is strategic deck/army composition rather than direct unit control — the tension lives in pre-battle decisions, not real-time micromanagement.

**Inspirations:** Epic War (flash series) — single-lane castle siege format, auto-marching units, mana-gated deployment.

**Current scope:** Single-player, single-lane 2D battlefield. No multiplayer or monetization considerations at this stage — those are explicitly out of scope for this document until (if ever) the project reaches a point where they're relevant.

**Setting & tone:** Undecided — see [Section 6](#6-setting--tone). The mechanical loop below is intentionally setting-agnostic for now.
